About Us

LifeStream Blood Services is a non-profit blood bank organization that provides blood and blood products to hospitals in the Greater Toronto Area. We are committed to providing a safe and adequate supply of blood, plasma, and related services to the patients of our partner hospitals.

Our mission is to provide a safe and adequate supply of blood, plasma, and related services to the patients of our partner hospitals.

Why Donate Blood?

  • Save lives
  • Free mini-physical to learn more about your own health
  • Reduce the risk of a heart attack, cancer and harmful iron deposits
  • Improve your mental state and engage with your community

Did you Know?

  • Every 60 seconds, someone in Canada needs blood.
  • One in two Canadians are eligible to donate blood.
  • More than 38,000 blood donations are needed every day.
  • One donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • More than 1.6 million people were diagnosed with cancer last year. Many of them will need blood, sometimes daily, during their chemotherapy treatment.

Donation Tips

  1. Get a good night's sleep.
  2. Have a healthy meal and drink fluids within four hours before donating. Avoid fatty foods, such as hamburgers, fries or ice cream before donating.
  3. Wear clothing with sleeves that can be raised above the elbow.
  4. Bring a list of medications you are taking.
  5. Remember to bring your donor card, driver's license or two other forms of ID.
